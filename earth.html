Earth

Earth, our home planet, is the third planet in our solar system. It is a rocky, terrestrial planet covered in mountains, valleys, canyons, plains, and much more. It's a special planet because 70% of its surface is covered by water. We are able to live on Earth because of the abundance of water. It is also found within the perfect distance from the sun to be habitable. This area is called the Goldilocks zone.

Its atmosphere is composed mostly of nitrogen and oxygen that we can breathe. It is divided by into five layers (troposphere, stratosphere, mesosphere, thermosphere, exosphere) based on temperature. The atmosphere protects us from incoming meteoroids, because it disintegerates them before they hit the surface.
